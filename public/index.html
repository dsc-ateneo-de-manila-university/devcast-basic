DevCast: The Lead's Story

DevCast is the official podcast of Developer Student Clubs Loyola that features the stories of student leaders and professionals in the Google community. It discusses their successes, challenges, and valuable insights in their journey towards pursuing their passions in the field of technology.

Season One

Episode 1: Seizing the Opportunity to Lead in DSC

What does it take to establish and lead a DSC chapter? In this episode, we catch up with Bruce Alturas, the founder, former DSC Lead, and current Design Lead of Developer Student Clubs - University of San Carlos, and talk about his humble beginnings in discovering his passion for Design and Information Technology. He shares his journey in founding a DSC Chapter at his university, his strategies to become an effective leader, and how joining DSC made him more passionate in his career path. He imparts insightful tips as well to students who aim to develop their technical skills.

Episode 2: Overcoming Challenges in Transitioning to a Tech Degree from a Non-Programming Background

How does one stay motivated to go deeper in learning programming despite being new to it? In this episode, we catch up with Anike Dorgu, current DSC Lead of Central Philippine University Iloilo, and talk about her life as a Software Engineering student and how the challenges it brings convinced her to shift to it midway through her freshman year. She shares the ups and downs of being a tech student and how being optimistic can change one's mindset when developing one's technical skills. She imparts her insights on how to conquer the feeling of imposter syndrome and achieving progress by getting started in personal projects.

Episode 3: How to Maximize your Potential When Learning New Technologies

How can you efficiently utilize the vast learning resources on the Internet? Joshua joins us on DevCast to talk about why it is important to get started in coding projects to build a substantial project portfolio when applying for jobs or internships. He shares tips on how to learn new technologies while working without getting burnt out, and how to get ideas when one does not know exactly where to start in building a portfolio. He also talks about his role as a Google Developer Expert, how he became one at such a young age, and how this role has made an impact in his career and personal life.
